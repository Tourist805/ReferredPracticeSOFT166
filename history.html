      Story

    • Dating from around around 1300 BCE in ancient Egypt, people played on three-in-a-row boards.

    • Also in the Roman Empire, the reserchears found variaton of tic tac toe game, around the first century BC. It was called terni lapilli (three pebbles at a time) and instead of having any number of pieces, each player only had three, thus they had to move them around to empty spaces to keep playing.

      • Game situation tree

    • A tree of game situations for the game of tic-tac-toe, where the player for the "tic-tac-toe" goes first and acts according to the above algorithm, and the player for the "tac-toe" can act as he pleases another), consists of 50 nodes

      • Computer solution

    • To solve this kind of games, a tree of game situations is built on the computer in accordance with the mini-max method. The total number of nodes in such a tree is 255168. This number is obtained as the sum of all possible options for moves - 9 options at the first step, 8 - for each of 9 at the second step, 7 - at each of 72 options at the third step, etc. , excluding situations of early end of the game (winning)
